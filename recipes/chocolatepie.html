Chocolate Pudding Pie

Simple but decadent

This Easy Chocolate Pie is so delicious, but not overly sweet. The recipe is simple, so the hardest part is waiting for it to set!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 recipe (1 disk) All-Butter Pie Dough
  • 115g bittersweet chocolate, coursely choped
  • 3 large eggs
  • 657ml cream
  • 20g cornflower
  • 150g sugar
  • 15g cocoa powder
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons kosher salt or heaped 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
